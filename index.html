About Me

Hello, world! My name is Brandon Gautier. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computuing Science and Information Technology at Athabasca University. In addition to Computing Science, I am also highly interested in the field of Psychology. In fact, I have a Bachelor's Degree (BSc) in Psychology from the University of Alberta. My senior undergraduate project involved the use of animal models to study various neuroprotective methods against stroke.

I also possess a Master's Degree (MSc) in Neuropsychology from the University of Edinburgh. My Master's dissertation focused on the impact of planning on multitasking abilities in young and old adults. Outside of academics, I enjoy practicing the guitar, working out, and playing computer games.
